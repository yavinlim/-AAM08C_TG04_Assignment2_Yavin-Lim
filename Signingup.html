Signing up

Where to begin


  • 1. Download the ActiveSG mobile application here
  • 2. Follow the steps on the picture to the right for signing up
  • 3. After signing up, click on the facilities tab at the bottom left and search for "Gym"
  • 4. Book your desired timing to work out and check out to pay
  • 5. Don't be a no-show and start exercising!

    • Extra
    Information

  • 1. With effect from 15 September 2021, the timing for the new release of gym slots for booking will be revised to 9am daily.

  • 2. Cancellation of Gym/Pool Bookings – With effect from 14 January 2021, members are able to cancel their gym/pool bookings up to 2 hours before the start of the timeslot. No refunds are provided for cancellations. Cancelled bookings do not count towards the no-show quota.

  • 3. Extension of Gym Timeslot – With effect from 14 January 2021, duration of gym slot is increased from 90 to 100 minutes.

  • 4. No-Show Policy - With effect from 1 October 2020, members who do not turn up for three or more gym/pool bookings will have their membership suspended for two weeks. Members will be notified via email should their account be suspended. During the period of suspension, members will not be allowed to book any of our ActiveSG facilities.

  • 5. 3-Day Advance Booking - With effect from 1 October 2020, to ensure that all members have equal opportunities to exercise at our facilities, members are able to book gym/pool slots only up to three days in advance.
